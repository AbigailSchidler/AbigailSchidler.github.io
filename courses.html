Selected research presentations

Proactive Conversational Agents with Inner Thoughts
Xingyu Bruce Liu, Shitao Fang, Weiyan Shi, Chien-Sheng Wu, Takeo Igarashi, Xiang ‘Anthony’ Chen 2025

This paper explored how intrinsically motivated “inner thoughts” can help conversational agents contribute proactively instead of waiting for prompts, which changed how I think about designing AI systems as active collaborators rather than passive tools.

Exploring AI-Based Support in Speech-Language Pathology for Culturally and Linguistically Diverse Children
Aeleyah Lewis, Aayushi Dangol, Hyewon Suh, Abbie Olszewski, James FogartyMy amazing professor, Julie A. Kientz 2025

This paper examined how bias and representation gaps affect AI-supported speech-language tools for culturally and linguistically diverse children, reinforcing the importance of careful prompting and source verification, which is an approach I later incorporated into Paperflow by linking generated content back to its original sections.
