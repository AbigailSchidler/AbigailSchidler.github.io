Music & Composition

Composing is a different kind of thinking — structural and intuitive at the same time. It's where I explore ideas that don't fit neatly into code.

I approach music the way I approach software: iteratively, with close attention to structure, and with intuition about when the rules serve the work and when they don't. The two practices reinforce each other more than I expected.

Right now this page is early. I'm working on adding original compositions, analytical writing, and projects that sit at the intersection of music and technology.
